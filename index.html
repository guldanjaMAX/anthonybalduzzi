Who I Am

I am the mass and I am the fire beneath it.

I lost my father to cancer when I was nine years old. He was forty two. That loss became the furnace that forged everything I have built since. It put me on a path of medicine, of understanding the body, of trying to save the lives that could not be saved in my own home.

I became a naturopathic doctor. I built health companies that have helped tens of thousands of men and women reclaim their vitality. For over fourteen years, Fit Father Project and Fit Mother Project have been my life's work in the wide circle, the work of scale, of community, of reaching as many people as possible.

But there has always been a deeper current running beneath the surface. In my late twenties, I ignored an intuition to slow down on a ski slope and slammed into a tree at full speed. Shattered leg. Broken arm. Nearly died. What followed was a decade of reconstruction: seven surgeries, including a leg lengthening procedure, and a parallel spiritual awakening. I went to the Amazon and drank ayahuasca. I sat in total darkness for a week. I found what I had been searching for since I was nine: a direct, unshakable connection to God.

I became a father to my daughter Brooke. I went through a divorce. I rebuilt again. And through all of it, the through line became clear: every breaking was an opening, and every opening brought me closer to center. I am not just a doctor. I am a follower of Yeshua. A man who has been broken and rebuilt more times than most people would believe. And now, I am being called to work with men at the deepest level I know how to reach.

Every breaking was an opening. Every opening brought me closer to center.

The Path

Forged by loss. Rebuilt by fire.

1996

The First Fire

At nine years old, I watched cancer take my father. He was forty two. In that loss, a furnace was lit inside me. I made a vow I did not fully understand yet: I would learn how to heal. I would learn how to keep people alive.

2005

The Body as Teacher

I turned my pain into physical discipline. Competitive bodybuilding taught me what it means to sculpt something from suffering, to use the body as a container for will. It was not the destination, but it was the training ground.

2012

Fit Father Project

What began as a mission became a movement. Fit Father Project and later Fit Mother Project grew into companies serving tens of thousands of families. The wide circle was expanding. I was helping men reclaim their vitality, their energy, their lives.

2017

The Tree

I ignored an intuition to slow down on a ski slope and slammed into a tree at full speed. Shattered leg. Broken arm. Nearly died. Seven surgeries over the next decade, including a leg lengthening procedure. In the wreckage, something clarified. The body is not the whole story. There is something deeper holding us together that no surgeon's hand can reach.

2019

The Amazon

A plant medicine journey in the Amazon opened a door I did not know existed. In the jungle, with the fire and the medicine and the silence, I met parts of myself I had been running from my whole life. I sat in total darkness for a week. I stopped running. I found a direct, personal, felt communion with God that was mine alone.

Now

The Inner Circle

The wide circle still turns. Fit Father and Fit Mother continue to serve. But the innermost circle is opening. Sacred, slow, one man at a time. I am being called to bring other men to the center I have found. This is the work I was built for. The work that requires all of me.

I am not speaking from theory. I am speaking from scars.

The Work

This is not coaching. This is fire.

I work with men who have already succeeded by the world's standards and are dying of tameness on the inside. Men who sense that something deeper is calling them but cannot name it. Men who are ready to stop performing and start living from the center of who they actually are. The work begins with an in person meeting. You come to me, or I come to you. From there, we undertake a deep exploration across five phases.

I

Physical Alignment

We train the body. Jaw to spine alignment, hanging, moving, twisting. Cardiovascular activation, nitric oxide. Cleaning the digestive tract. Living foods. Connecting daily routine with natural alignment. Strength, agility, full embodied vitality. The body is the first honest mirror.

II

Spiritual Exploration

We explore the deepest questions: Who are you? Where do you come from? Why are you here? What is the alignment from your center? We develop your direct relationship with God, one that is real, yours, and direct. Not inherited. Not performative. Yours.

III

Ceremony

At the right point in the work, we go into nature, by the fire. You take sacred medicine in a legal container. I play the flute. You go deep within yourself. I hold space. This is not recreational. This is the ancient work of meeting yourself in the dark and coming back whole.

IV

Life Alignment

We examine your relationships, your fears, your mission, your daily patterns. We do the forgiveness work, the clearing, the rebuilding. We plan adventures. We make radical shifts where they are needed. At the midpoint, I come to your home. We do it together.

V

Integration and Release

I teach you my system. The movements, the nutrition, the stillness practices, the prayer life, the daily alignment. May it serve you for life. The work has an ending. I am not building dependency. I transmit what I know and send you back into your life, transformed and free.

Recognition

You already know if this is for you.

Has built the life he was supposed to want and knows something essential is missing. Who feels a deeper, wilder call in his spirit that he has become tame against. The weight of responsibility and routine has boxed him in. His physical health is nowhere near where he wants it to be.

He craves adventure but is stuck in a loop. He wants to be more connected with God but it has felt performative. He is escaping: doom scrolling, self medicating, maybe drinking too much, eating poorly. He feels the misalignment but does not have the tools or the guide to address it.

He is not broken. He is caged. And he is ready to break out.

This is not for the man who...

Is looking for optimization hacks, biohacking shortcuts, or performance coaching. Who is skeptical of or hostile to God, spirituality, or the sacred. Who is not open to plant medicine work. Who wants a guru to follow rather than doing his own deep work.

Who is not ready to be radically honest with himself. Who wants to outsource his transformation to someone else. Who cannot commit the time, financial investment, and emotional availability this work demands.

This is mentorship and spiritual guidance, not therapy. Not mainstream fitness. Not a weekend retreat. This is the full, sacred work of becoming who you actually are.
