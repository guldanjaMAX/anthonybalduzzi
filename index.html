About Dr. Anthony Balduzzi

Dr. Anthony Balduzzi is a naturopathic doctor, guide, and seeker dedicated to helping men return to a state of aliveness, strength, connection, and power from center. With years of experience in plant medicine, somatic practices, and sacred mentorship, Dr. Balduzzi offers a unique approach to men's health and personal transformation.

His work combines ancient wisdom with modern science, creating personalized pathways for men to reconnect with their authentic selves, strengthen their bodies, deepen their relationships, and tap into their full potential.
